SCDC in Placements

At Sree Chaitanya Degree College, we just don’t focus on practical and quality education but also on the establishments of out students’ careers. We train our students to inculcate the industry standard soft skills and communication skills and allow them to display their performance in interviews. So far we have arranged one mega off campus recruitment program with a pool of companies. Students from SCDC have proved it there by getting selected in 50 positions out of 300 whole selections made by all the companies.

Our Placements Polacy

To place every student in a good position in national and multinational companies by the end of their course through campus recruitment training programs that pounds its importance on molding students ready to hire and get into work.
